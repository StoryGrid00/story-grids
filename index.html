Step 1: One-Sentence Summary

Write a one-sentence summary of your novel (15 words or less). This is your elevator pitch.

Step 2: One-Paragraph Summary

Expand your sentence into a paragraph describing the story setup, major disasters, and ending.

Step 3: Character Summaries

Create one-page summaries for each major character.

Step 5: Character Synopses

Write detailed synopses for each major character.
